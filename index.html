About the Company


The KION Group is expanding its corporate profile in the key growth market of India: KION India with its OM and OM Voltas brand is one of the country's leading suppliers of forklift trucks and warehouse technology. KION India sells both electric and IC forklift trucks as well as warehouse technology products under the established Voltas and Baoli as well as Linde Material Handling brands in India.
